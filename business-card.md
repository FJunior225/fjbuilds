## Design / print notes

- No logo — clean typography beats a rushed logo.
- One accent color max. Lots of white space. No robot/brain clip art.
- Stock: heavier matte or soft-touch so it survives in a stack of 40.
- Sizes: standard US 3.5" x 2".
- Vendors: Moo (premium stock), Vistaprint (cheap/fast), FedEx Office (same-day backup).
- Order by Saturday to have them before the June 25 event.
